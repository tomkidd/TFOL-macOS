Enabling or disabling Fullscreen Antialiasing (FSAA)

To enable or disable the "multisample" extension (aka "FSAA"), go to the Quake II console and set the variable "gl_arb_multisample" to 0, 4 or 8. F.e. "gl_arb_multisample 8" (without the quotes) will set the sample level to 8. Changing the sample level will force a restart of the OpenGL video driver.

Note: You wil need at least MacOS X v10.1 if you are using a NVidia Geforce 3 or 4Ti. ATI Radeon users will need at least MacOS X v10.2.
